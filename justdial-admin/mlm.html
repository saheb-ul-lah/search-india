Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our network marketing opportunity

How much does it cost to join?

The joining fee is ₹500, which gives you access to our distributor portal, training materials, and your unique referral code. To activate your account, you'll need to make a minimum purchase of ₹500.

How do I earn money?

You earn through multiple income streams: direct sales commissions, team commissions from your downline (up to 10 levels), leadership bonuses, royalty income, and rank advancement rewards. The more you sell and the larger your network grows, the more you earn.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is required. We provide comprehensive training and support to help you succeed. Our step-by-step training program covers product knowledge, sales techniques, team building, and leadership development.

How do I get paid?

Commissions are calculated weekly and monthly, and paid directly to your e-wallet in the distributor portal. You can request withdrawals to your bank account after completing KYC verification.

Is this a legitimate business?

Yes, EK Network is a legitimate direct selling company that follows all legal guidelines. We focus on product sales and building a sustainable business, not on recruitment alone. Our compensation plan rewards actual product sales.

How much time do I need to invest?

The beauty of this business is its flexibility. You can work part-time or full-time based on your goals and availability. Many successful distributors start part-time and transition to full-time as their business grows.
